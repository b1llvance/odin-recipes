The Perfect Ham Sandwich

Description

I like to think the perfect lunchtime meal is a ham sandwich with all the veggies and a side of kettle chips. This is a recipe that can be thrown together in just a few minutes and eaten at home, taken to work for a nice homemade desk lunch or even 1 handed on the run.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your choice of bread - my go to is classic Wonder white bread
  • 3 oz- Fresh deli slices of smoked honey ham
  • Sliced Heirloom tomato
  • Slice of Havarti cheese
  • Handful of Arugula
  • 1 Tbsp Brown mustard

Steps

  1. Place ham on one slice of bread
  2. Place cheese on the second slice of bread
  3. Spread brown mustard on the cheese slice
  4. Add sliced tomato, and arugula on top of the ham,
  5. Combine sandwich and slice diagonally
  6. Add a side of your favorite kettle chips
